Meet the Team

Sidhant Srivastava

Investment Strategy

Formerly involved with financial management, Sidhant takes genuine pride in his active coordination and development of SocTech’s strategy. Certainly, as our school’s Math Honor Society Vice President, Sidhant has developed a unique knack for problem solving, enabling him to contribute to an investment approach for Ms. Sohoni that effectively mitigates risk and maximizes on economic opportunities. As a freshman last year, Sidhant qualified for nationals in the Future Business Leaders of America club, and hopes to further entrench his interest in business with the opportunistic platform provided by Knowledge@Wharton High School.

Aditya Desai

Investment Strategy and Financial Literacy

With former experience in investment banking and business development, Aditya brings critical investment to SocTech’s strategy for each client. Whether it be determining asset allocation ratios, or stock distribution on an individual basis, he is committed to doing whatever it takes to produce tangible results for the firm. Aside from investments, Aditya is passionate about mobilizing financial literacy as a mechanism to disrupt the status quo. This innate motivation pushes him forward each day—leading him to serve as the National Director of Financial Literacy for the Future Business Leaders of America, and the founder of his own nonprofit.

Edward Wang

Technological Development

An avid problem solver, and mathematics enthusiast, Edward brings tremendous nuance to our group through his computational experience. Specifically, as a former qualifier to the American Invitational Mathematics Exam (AIME) and competitor in the Harvard-MIT Mathematics Tournament (HMMT), Edward has honed his ability to confront conflicts, and subsequently overcome them with a developed approach. At SocTech, Edward focuses on the development of our social reputation algorithm, which utilizes a natural learning processor to analyze relevant news and tweet regarding a company—to ultimately corroborate our quantitative and qualitative findings.

Jesse Li

Investment Strategy

Aspiring to immerse himself in the financial field, while bringing his current knowledge to the forefront, Jesse joined SocTech, and currently contributes to the development of its investment strategy. Of course, his former involvement in the Princeton University Mathematics Competition has set a fundamental foundation for his passion for computational finance. As a result, Jesse strives to create financial models and asset allocation approaches to further the success of SocTech, at large.

Chengnan Li

Website Development

From an early onset, Chengnan has been fascinated by technology and its inner workings, leading him to lead website development for SocTech. Notably, Chengnan isn’t new to web design; over the last summer, he pursued an independent research project in computer science with a mentor at a local university. Beyond providing an insightful immersion, the experience has furthered Chengnan’s interest in technological facets such that he routinely engages in online programming classes. Throughout the process, Chengnan is excited to familiarize himself with the finance sector, and focus on its intersection with technology.
