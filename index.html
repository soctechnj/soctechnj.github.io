About Us

At Soctech, we are dedicated to catering diversified investment portfolios to our clients—specialized and entrenched to capitalize on all economic opportunities. Our analysts pride themselves on crafting exclusive investment strategies, on a case-by-case basis to ultimately harness prosperity for our clients. Indeed, each asset selection necessitates a specialized account, on our behalf, of a client’s investment preferences, risk tolerance, and age.

Our Vision

While the asset management industry has become consolidated by multinational conglomerates, what sets us apart is our integrity, dedication and implementation of modern technology to produce true societal benefits—for both our clients and the worlds around them. Our Strategy

Whereas our approach varies from client to client, Soctech utilizes a definitive acrostic as a baseline approach. Naturally, we’ve transformed our popular view as an oasis into a proposition of investing: OASIS—Overview, Asset selection, Sector selection, Industry selection, and Stock selection and weighting. Within each encompassing category, our analysts evaluate a series of fundamental and technical factors to hone each strategy accordingly.
